Why Decentralized Sewage Treatment?

Traditional centralized sewage treatment requires expensive infrastructure, extensive pipelines, and large-scale investments. In contrast, decentralized treatment brings the solution closer to the source of wastewater generation, reducing costs, increasing efficiency, and ensuring sustainability.
